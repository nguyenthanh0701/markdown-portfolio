Shirakawago

  • Home
  • Destinations
  • Takayama
  • Shirakawago
Experience a sense of majestic nature in this historic village.

With its unique local houses with their pointy straw roofs, Shirakawa-go (UNESCO World Heritage listed) is already a prime destination for tourists. The village still preserves its traditional way of living and the techniques to survive through the severe snowfall in winter. Stay overnight or visit for a day.
